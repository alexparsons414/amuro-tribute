Amuro Ray

Child solider to hero of the One Year War to Savior of Earth

Major Events Revolving around Amuro

  • U.C. 0063 - Born on Earth to Tem and Kamaria Ray
  • U.C. 0079 - Becomes the pilot of the RX-78-2, the Earth Federation's first prototype mobile suit. Together with a group of other teenagers and a commanding officer of little experience White Base plays a major role in the One Year War. Eventually he awakens as a Newtype.
  • U.C. 0087 - Joins the A.E.U.G. forces in the Gryps conflict against the Titans forming an unlikely truce with Char Aznable, a key enemy during the One Year War.
  • U.C. 0088 - Joins in the First Neo Zeon War against the Axis powers led by Haman Karn
  • U.C. 0093 - During the Second Neo Zeon War against the Axis powers led by Char Aznable, Amuro sacrifices himself to stop Char's plan to drop the Gryps 2 meteor onto the earth causing a new Ice Age, both Amuro and Char disappear.
